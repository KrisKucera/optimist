The Optimist Creed

Promise Yourself

  1. To be so strong that nothing can disturb your peace of mind.
  2. To talk health, happiness and prosperity to every person you meet.
  3. To make all your friends feel that there is something in them.
  4. To look at the sunny side of everything and make your optimism come true.
  5. To think only of the best, to work only for the best, and to expect only the best.
  6. To be just as enthusiastic about the success of others as you are about your own.
  7. To forget the mistakes of the past and press on to the greater achievements of the future.
  8. To wear a cheerful countenance at all times and give every living creature you meet a smile.
  9. To give so much time to the improvement of yourself that you have no time to criticize others.
  10. To be too large for worry, too noble for anger, too strong for fear, and too happy to permit the presence of trouble.

At Your Service

Tri-Star Basketball

Every January we host the Optimist Tri-Star basketball skills test. Com join us and the knights of Columbus at the Solon Community center.

National Honor Society Tea

The optimist club hosts the annual National Honor Society tea for honor society induction and their families.

Easter Egg Hunt

Every Spring we put out over 3,000 eggs for those youngsters to track down during our annual Easter Egg hunt. All participants get a prize!

Beef Days Fishing Derby

During Beef Days come join us for the Annual fishing derby at the Brosh pond.

Essay Contest

This annual event is a way for high schoolers to win scholarships for their writing skills. This is an annual event sponsored by Optimist International and our local chapter.

Oratorical Contest

This annual event is a way for high schoolers to win scholarships for their oratory skills.

Bike Rodeo

This annual event lets young riders test the bicycling skills. Watch for this event in mid-June.

School Support

We support our schools. We have provided funds for projects such as Win with Reading, the Writer workshop, we provided funds towards the new Grand Piano in the local center for the arts and many other school groups.

Beef Days Bingo

BINGO! Come play a few rounds of Bingo during beef days. We have been a long supporter of the Solon Beef Days celebration. This is one of our annual fund raisers to support local youth.

Seafood Sales

Our annual winter fund raiser. Order forms will be available in January. Purchase Seafood, Steaks or other delectable delights and help our club and the local youth.

Community Support

We support our community by providing funds for events such as Music on Main, Beef Days, Solon Parks and Rec and local youth groups.

Avenue of Flags

This is our newest fund raiser. Show you support to the USA and our Veterans by subscribing for a flag to be placed in your front Yard on the holidays of Memorial Day, Flag Day, July 4th, Labor Day and Veterans Day. Local groups may participate to earn money towards their favorite project.
